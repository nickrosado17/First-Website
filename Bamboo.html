Bamboo Chicken kabobs


Chicken Kabobs recipe requires just a few simple ingredients.

Tender, juicy chicken is marinated and grilled with peppers and onion and the entire dish can be made ahead of time.


Ingredients Needed:

  • Soy sauce
  • Sprite
  • Oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Horseradish
  • Chicken
  • Bell pepper
  • Onion

How To Make Chicken Kabobs:

  1. the marinade. Cut the chicken into 1 inch pieces and add it to a gallon size resealable bag. Marinate for 1-3 hours.
  2. Slice the vegetables..
  3. Prepare your skewers.. Thread the chicken and chopped vegetables alternately onto the wooden skewers, keeping a little space between each piece.
  4. Grill or oven cook.
